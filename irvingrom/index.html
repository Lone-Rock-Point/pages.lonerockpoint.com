03 · The Foundation

CivicPress. WordPress, purpose-built for government.

Lone Rock Point's WordPress framework integrates the U.S. Web Design System (USWDS) into WordPress. Born from our work modernizing NASA.gov to meet the 21st Century IDEA Act and OMB M-23-22. CivicPress is open. The City retains full ownership of its content, with no proprietary lock-in.

Meet Andy

AI-leveraged migration. Months of work, condensed and quality-controlled.

Andy is our in-house tool that combines automated content extraction, structured mapping, and AI-assisted review. It dramatically reduces manual effort on large migrations while preserving content fidelity, URL structure, and metadata.

  1. Audit and inventory. Every page, document, and asset cataloged with current URL, owner, and disposition.
  2. Automated migration with AI assist. Andy handles bulk transfer into the new CivicPress structure.
  3. Manual QA and staff review. City content owners verify formatting, accuracy, and accessibility before go-live.
  4. URL preservation and 301 redirects. Existing URLs preserved where possible.
  5. Tested rollback protocol. Backup and restore procedures ensure a zero-data-loss launch.
