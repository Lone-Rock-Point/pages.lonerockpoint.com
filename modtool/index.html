04 / How It Works

Assessment Pipeline

Stages 1 through 4: Collect and prepare
01
Crawl & Inventory
Spider target sites to build a complete page inventory with metadata.
  • Follows robots.txt and sitemap.xml directives
  • Records HTTP status codes, redirects, response times
  • Builds page hierarchy and internal link graph
02
Extract & Clean
Parse HTML to extract meaningful text, stripping boilerplate and navigation.
  • Removes headers, footers, navs, and sidebars
  • Extracts main content body and metadata
  • Identifies content blocks vs. chrome
03
Freshness Checks
Assess content currency and identify stale or misconfigured redirects.
  • Checks last-modified dates and published timestamps
  • Flags pages older than configurable threshold
  • Validates redirect chains (301/302)
04
Duplicate Detection
Find redundant content using fingerprinting and similarity analysis.
  • Generates content fingerprints via SimHash / MinHash
  • Clusters near-duplicate pages by similarity score
  • Identifies canonical vs. duplicate candidates
04 / How It Works

Assessment Pipeline

Stages 5 through 8: Analyze and report
05
Readability Scoring
Score content for readability using established indices and plain language criteria.
  • Flesch-Kincaid, Gunning Fog, Dale-Chall, SMOG
  • Flags jargon, passive voice, complex sentences
  • Benchmarks against target reading level
06
LEP / Language Coverage
Detect language availability and assess coverage gaps for LEP audiences.
  • Identifies available language versions per page
  • Maps coverage gaps against target languages
  • Checks translation quality signals
07
Governance & Transparency
Verify compliance with governance requirements and transparency standards.
  • Checks for required disclosures and disclaimers
  • Validates privacy and accessibility statements
  • Assesses metadata completeness
08
Score & Report
Aggregate findings into maturity scores and generate actionable reports.
  • Calculates overall and category maturity scores (1 to 5)
  • Prioritises findings by impact and effort
  • Generates exportable report with recommendations
